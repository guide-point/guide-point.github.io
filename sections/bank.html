Bank Account Setup Guide

Find the best banks and learn how to open an account as a student in India.

Which Banks Allow Foreign Students?

Foreign students can open accounts in these banks:

  • 🏦 SBI (State Bank of India)
  • 🏦 Bank of Maharashtra
  • 🏦 Canara Bank

Required Documents

Obtain both a copy and the original, of all the following documents:

  • 📄 Passport
  • 📄 Visa
  • 📄 FRRO Registration
  • 📄 University Admission Letter
  • 📄 Bonafide
  • 📄 Printed passport Photos [ 4 - 6 Photos ]
  • 📄 Address Proof [ Agreement , C-Form ..etc]
  • 📄 Make a cash deposit, usually $2,000 INR —you’ll get it back in your account.

Steps to Open an Account

  1. 📌 Choose a bank & visit the nearest branch.
  2. 📌 Fill out the account opening form.
  3. 📌 Submit the required documents.
  4. 📌 Receive your account details & debit card. [in 1-2 weeks ]
