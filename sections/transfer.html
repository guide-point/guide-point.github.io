📌 Important Notes When Receiving Money in India

  • ✅ Always double-check the sender's information and amount before confirming any transaction.
  • ✅ Keep your government-issued ID (passport, Aadhaar, or visa) handy for in-person pickups.
  • ✅ Use official platforms or authorized partners — avoid suspicious third-party apps or links.
  • ✅ Be aware of fees and exchange rates — some services may deduct charges before delivering the money.
  • ✅ International transfers above ₹7,00,000 may attract tax collected at source (TCS) under Indian regulations.
  • ✅ SWIFT transfers may take several days — plan accordingly if the funds are time-sensitive.
  • ❗ For crypto transfers via Binance P2P, always verify the buyer/seller rating and avoid sharing personal OTPs.
